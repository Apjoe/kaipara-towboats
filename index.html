About Kaipara Tow Boats

Kaipara Tow Boats is a family-owned business established in 1988, providing harbour towage and workboat services within the Auckland area.

The company has expanded its fleet of tugs and barges with increasing demand, as well as the area of operations. Since its formation, the company has established a reputation as a reliable, safe quality towage operator.

With a good track record and high levels of skill, coupled with an honest can-do attitude, Kaipara Tow Boats maintains its fleet of vessels to a high standard, available 24/7.
